Interns

We are happy to introduce our class of 2020 interns who were selected through a rigorous application process. This internship opportunity is in collaboration with the Global Change Program (GCP), the Center for Career Discovery & Development (C2D2), and the Student Government Association (SGA).

Our internships have generously been funded and supported by our Serve-Learn-Sustain (SLS) partner at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

The Smart Sea Level Sensor internship will provide real-life experience to our interns to learn about climate change challenges and develop ideas on how to mitigate them.

You can find out more about our interns by clicking on their photos.
